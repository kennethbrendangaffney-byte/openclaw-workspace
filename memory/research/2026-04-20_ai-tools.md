## 🤖 Local & Self-Hosted AI

### Large Language Models (Local)
- **Ollama** - Still the dominant local LLM runner; supports Qwen, Llama, Mistral families
- **LM Studio** - GUI-based local inference with good model management
- **llama.cpp** - Core engine for efficient CPU/GPU inference
- **BitNet** - Ternary quantization (1.58-bit) enabling large models on consumer hardware
  - Microsoft BitNet-b1.58-2B-4T: 1.1GB RAM, ~27 t/s on CPU
  - Significant for edge deployment and privacy-conscious users

### Model Families Worth Watching
- **Qwen 2.5** (Alibaba) - Strong multilingual performance, good for coding
- **Llama 3.x** (Meta) - Reliable general-purpose, extensive ecosystem
- **Mistral** - Efficient, good at reasoning tasks
- **DeepSeek** - Cost-effective, strong coding capabilities
- **Kimi k2.5** (Moonshot) - Long context (2M tokens), excellent for document analysis

## 🚀 Emerging Trends (2026)

1. **Agentic AI** - Tools that can take actions, not just generate text
2. **Multimodal Everything** - Text, image, audio, video in single models
3. **Local-First** - Privacy concerns driving on-device inference
4. **Small Models** - Efficient models for specific tasks (1B-3B parameters)
5. **Reasoning Models** - Step-by-step thinking before answering
6. **AI-Native IDEs** - Not just copilots, but full AI-driven development
