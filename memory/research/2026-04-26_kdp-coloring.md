## 4. Publishing Strategy & Technical Specs

### KDP Technical Non-Negotiables

| Spec | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Bleed** | Add 0.125" (3mm) to outer edges |
| **Resolution** | 300 DPI (600+ can cause resizing artifacts) |
| **Safe Margins** | Keep critical content 0.325" inside trim line |
| **Standard Page** | 8.5" × 11" with 0.5" margins |
| **Line Weight** | 2–4 pixels wide for optimal printing |

### UX Best Practices

- **Single-sided printing** — prevents marker bleed-through (top 1-star review complaint).
- Add professional note on reverse: *"This page intentionally left blank to avoid bleed-through."*
- Include varied complexity levels in same book for broader appeal.
- Ensure adequate white space — avoid overcrowding.

### Pricing Strategy (3-Phase)

| Phase | Price Range | Goal |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **Launch** | $5.99 – $6.99 | Drive velocity, acquire first reviews |
| **Monitoring** | Watch print costs | Amazon fee hikes can erase margins |
| **Profit** | $8.99+ | ~$2.00/unit royalty once social proof established |

- **Adult niches** support premium pricing; **toddler books** are a race to the bottom.
- **Sweet spot:** $6.99 – $12.99 for most coloring books.
- KDP royalty: 35% or 70% depending on price point and distribution.

### Marketing & Launch

1. **Proof copy hacking:** Order author copies at cost, gift to friends/influencers for reviews (no "Verified Purchase" needed).
2. **First 5 reviews > royalties in week 1.** Obsess over reviews, not sales.
3. **Social proof:** Encourage users to post colored pages (UGC is strongest conversion driver).
4. **Pinterest & Instagram** = best platforms for visual products.
5. **Email list:** Offer free sample pages to build subscriber base for repeat launches.
6. **Amazon A10 algorithm** favors consistent sales velocity + positive reviews + keyword optimization.

### Series & Bundling Strategy

- Create **themed series** within a niche.
- Offer individual books at standard price + multi-book collections at discount.
- Target **3–5 related long-tail keywords** per niche for expansion room.
- Consider **international markets** — European & Asian demand for English coloring books is strong.

## 6. Key Takeaways for Ken

1. **Start with "Bold & Easy" or "Large Print" niches** — they dominate KDP, have lower competition, and serve an underserved audience (seniors, beginners, low vision).
2. **Pick ONE niche, go deep.** Don't scatter across 10 topics. Build a series.
3. **Quality is the moat in 2026.** The market is saturated with AI slop. Clean lines, proper margins, and professional presentation win reviews.
4. **Local AI generation is viable.** With your Linux rig, you could run Stable Diffusion + ControlNet locally for zero ongoing art costs. Worth exploring.
5. **Education > side hustle.** This research is solid background, but remember — CCT AI diploma and Trinity TAP are the priority. KDP can run in the background once systems are set up.
6. **Budget for tools:** ~$15–$25/month for a commercial AI coloring generator is reasonable if you want speed over local setup complexity.
