## 📚 Module Breakdown (8 Modules)

### 1. Strategic Business & Professional Development A
- Role of IT professionals
- Communicating technical information
- Choosing tools for organisational needs
- Professional codes of practice
- Foundation for all other modules

### 2. Ethics, Integrity and Bias in AI
- Ethical frameworks for AI
- Bias detection and mitigation
- Transparency and fairness
- Accountability in AI systems
- **Critical for modern AI roles**

### 3. Principles of Programming
- Fundamental programming concepts
- Problem-solving through code
- Underpins all technical modules
- **Likely Python-focused** (industry standard for AI)

### 4. AI Concepts for Problem Solving
- Definition and history of AI
- Different AI levels and approaches
- Real-world problem-solving scenarios
- Selecting appropriate AI solutions
- **Core theoretical foundation**

### 5. Machine Learning Tools
- Knowledge representation schemes
- ML methods and learning types
- Tools and languages for AI/ML
- Design decision-making
- **Practical hands-on module**

### 6. Visualisation Techniques for AI
- Data communication and insight
- Charts, dashboards, visual analytics
- Presenting complex data clearly
- **Important for stakeholder communication**

### 7. Big Data Analytical Methods
- Large-scale data storage
- SQL and NoSQL databases
- Processing and analytics at scale
- Building scalable ML solutions
- **Pipeline-focused**

### 8. Strategic Business & Professional Development B
- Advanced professional contexts
- Complex problem investigation
- Tool and technique application
- Culmination of professional skills

## 🔍 Key Differentiators

### ✅ Strengths
1. **Microsoft Partnership** — Curriculum aligned with industry needs
2. **QQI Level 7** — Nationally recognised qualification, progression pathway
3. **60 ECTS** — Full academic credit for further study
4. **Part-time Flexible** — Evening/weekend fits around life
5. **Free for Jobseekers** — No financial barrier
6. **Real-world Focus** — Problem-solving, not just theory
7. **Career Support** — Beyond academics: job search, coaching
8. **Progression Route** — Can advance to Level 8/9 at CCT

### ⚠️ Considerations
1. **Level 7 is Intermediate** — Not a full degree; entry-level/junior roles targeted
2. **Intensive Schedule** — Two evenings + Saturdays requires commitment
3. **Self-directed Learning** — Online component requires discipline
4. **College Reputation** — Specialist college, not university brand name
5. **No Deep Specialisation** — Broad coverage, not deep ML engineering
