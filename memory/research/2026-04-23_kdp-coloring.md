## 1. Market Overview (2026)

- **Global adult coloring book market**: ~$4.8 billion in 2025, with Amazon KDP accounting for ~45% of total sales.
- **Adult coloring books now represent >60%** of all coloring book sales on Amazon.
- **Therapeutic coloring categories grew 127%** year-over-year (mindfulness, stress relief, anxiety).
- **Q4 (Oct–Dec)** is the dominant sales period — holiday gifting drives massive spikes.
- **Bold & Easy** style is the dominant format across most niches (thick outlines, simple shapes, works with any coloring tool).

### Key Technical Requirements for AI Output
- **300 DPI minimum** — anything lower prints blurry.
- **Grayscale / pure B&W** — do NOT submit RGB; KDP conversion creates muddy lines.
- **Line weight: 2–8pt** — Bold & Easy uses 4–8pt for clean reproduction.
- **File format: PNG or TIFF** at 300 DPI per page, assembled into PDF.

## 4. KDP Publishing Strategy & Technical Specs

### Format Standards

| Spec | Recommendation |
|------|---------------|
| **Trim size (adult)** | 8.5" × 11" (letter) — most popular, expected format |
| **Trim size (kids)** | 8.5" × 8.5" (square) — lower print cost, simpler designs |
| **Page count** | 40 designs = 80 interior pages (single-sided rule) |
| **Bleed** | 0.125" on all sides — PDF page size becomes 8.75" × 11.25" |
| **Margins** | 0.375" minimum (top, bottom, outer, gutter) |
| **Color mode** | Grayscale for B&W interiors |

### The Single-Sided Rule
- Every coloring page needs a **blank backing page**.
- Structure: design (recto/right page), blank (verso/left page).
- 40 designs = 80 total pages. This prevents marker bleed-through and bad reviews.

### Pricing & Profit Math (Example)
- **80-page B&W interior, white paper**: $1.96 print cost (Amazon.com).
- **Price at $9.99** with 60% royalty = $5.99 royalty.
- **Profit per sale**: $5.99 − $1.96 = **$4.03**.
- Going to 50 designs (100 pages) adds cost but also perceived value.

### Cover Requirements
- Single PDF: back cover + spine + front cover (horizontal layout).
- Spine width = page count × 0.002252" (for B&W on white paper).
- Include 0.125" bleed on outer edges.
- **Cover matters more than interior** — big readable title, bright colors, kid-friendly fonts, sample images on back.

### Validation Checklist Before Publishing
1. Search target phrase on Amazon — check BSRs (under 100K = selling).
2. Count competition — aim for 500–5,000 results with strong BSRs.
3. Read 1-star reviews — identify gaps ("designs too small", "lines too thin", "not enough pages").
4. Check seasonal timing — launch holiday books 6–8 weeks before peak.

## 5. Workflow: From Idea to Published Book (Same Day)

1. **Niche validation** (15 min) — Amazon search + BSR check.
2. **Generate pages with AI** (5–30 min) — Use ColoringBook.ai, LipPal.ai, or LineForge.
3. **Format interior in Canva** (10 min) — Drag-and-drop into free KDP templates, export PDF.
4. **Design cover** (15 min) — Canva KDP cover template, bold title, bright colors.
5. **Upload to KDP** (10 min) — Create paperback, upload interior + cover, set pricing.
6. **Publish** — Book goes live worldwide, sells 24/7.

**Total time**: 1–2 hours for a complete book.

---

## 6. Key Insights & Recommendations

- **Bold & Easy dominates** — thick outlines, simple shapes, works with crayons/markers/colored pencils. Don't overcomplicate.
- **Seasonal timing is everything** — Q4 (Christmas, winter, advent) is where the money is. Launch 6–8 weeks early.
- **Niche down hard** — "Coastal cottagecore coloring book for adults" beats "Adult coloring book" every time.
- **Reviews matter less than you think** — many profitable books have <100 reviews. BSR and cover quality matter more.
- **Consistency beats perfection** — publish regularly, test niches, double down on what sells.
- **AI tools have matured** — 2026 tools can generate full 200-page books with consistent characters and story flow. Leverage this.
