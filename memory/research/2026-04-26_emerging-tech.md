## 🔬 Quantum Computing

### Major Milestones
- **ParityQC 52-Qubit Quantum Fourier Transform** (April 16, 2026): European quantum architecture company ParityQC set a new record implementing the largest Quantum Fourier Transform on 52 superconducting qubits using IBM hardware. This is a significant benchmark for quantum algorithms.
- **Rigetti 108-Qubit System Generally Available** (April 7, 2026): Rigetti announced general availability of their Cepheus-1-108Q system, validating their proprietary chiplet-based scaling architecture. Now accessible to customers and partners via the Rigetti Quantum Cloud.
- **Fermionic Atom Gates Exceed 99% Accuracy** (April 20, 2026): A long-sought milestone achieved — neutral atom quantum computing platforms now have collisional gates with accuracy exceeding 99%, making them viable for fault-tolerant quantum computing.
- **Protected Quantum Gates via Qubit Doublons**: New research in Nature (April 2026) demonstrates protected quantum gates using qubit doublons in dynamical optical lattices, advancing neutral atom platforms.

### Key Trend
Quantum computing is transitioning from research curiosity to commercial availability, with multiple platforms (superconducting, neutral atoms) hitting critical thresholds for error correction and scale.

## 🧬 Biotechnology & CRISPR

### Major Milestones
- **Editas Medicine CRISPR-Cas12a Functional Cure** (April 5, 2026): Published in NEJM — achieved functional cure in 27 of 28 sickle cell patients in the RUBY trial. This represents one of the most successful gene-editing therapies to date.
- **Cleveland Clinic Gene Editing Success** (April 1, 2026): Independent confirmation — gene editing therapy showed success against severe sickle cell disease with nearly all patients achieving functional cure.
- **CRISPR Moving to Personalized Medicine**: Two years after the first FDA approvals, CRISPR is expanding from single-gene disorders toward personalized medicine approaches, with improved delivery mechanisms and reduced off-target effects.

### Key Trend
Gene editing has moved from experimental to therapeutic reality. Sickle cell disease is becoming the proof point, with the field now expanding to more complex genetic conditions and personalized approaches.

SpaceX is executing on its Mars roadmap with hardware iterations (V3, Raptor 3) that dramatically improve cost and performance. 2026 is the year Mars missions transition from aspiration to actual launch manifests.

## 🧪 Novel Materials

### Major Milestones
- **Laser-Stepwise Induced Graphene (LSIG)** (April 9, 2026): Wuhan University of Technology developed a two-step laser process for creating graphene with tunable electromagnetic shielding and remarkably low sheet resistance. More accessible and controllable than traditional methods.
- **Graphene-VO2 Hybrid Metasurfaces**: Dynamically reconfigurable metamaterials combining graphene with vanadium dioxide enable switchable polarization conversion, multiband absorption, and programmable scattering control.
- **Terahertz Graphene Metamaterials**: New designs achieve ultra-high amplitude modulation depth for terahertz frequencies, opening applications in advanced communications and sensing.

### Key Trend
Graphene is finally moving from laboratory curiosity to tunable, manufacturable materials. The focus is on dynamic reconfigurability — materials that can change properties on demand for electromagnetic applications, shielding, and next-gen communications.
